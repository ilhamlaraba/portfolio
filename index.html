Questions you may ask

Yoga is your niche for copywriting?

No, yoga is a niche that I had no prior experience with. I chose it because it proves that I am prepared for any business or industry.

As a copywriter, how do you research?

I look for emotional connections between people and the industry. I seek industry trends and incorporate them into headlines and content as much as possible. I am concerned with value and I strive to discover it in the business. Of course, there are more.

How do you handle criticism to your copy?

I take criticism constructively and adjust my copy accordingly and learn from it.

What kind of work environment do you prefer?

I prefer a team environment where I can develop additional skills and knowledge.
